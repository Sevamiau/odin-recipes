Spinach and Feta Turkey Burgers

Receta de hamburguesa de espinaca con queso feta

Best burguer to enjoy this summer!

Ingredients:

  • Cooking spray
  • 2 pounds ground turkey
  • 1 (10 ounce) box frozen chopped spinach
  • 4 ounces feta chese
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Directions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Mix together turkey, spinach, feta, eggs, and garlic in a large bowl until well combined; form into 8 patties.
  3. Cook patties on the preheated grill on both sides until no longer pink in the center, 15 to 20 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center of patties should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)./li>
